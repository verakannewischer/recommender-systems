The Pipeline

Retrieve → Rank → Diversify

No single algorithm can do everything. We split the job into three stages, each covering the previous one's blind spot.

Define Your Material Palette

Tell us what aesthetic you're going for. Add materials like Oak, Brass, or Marble to build your project palette. SourceMatch creates a rich profile of your creative vision and retrieves every product that fits.

AI Learns What Works Together

Our collaborative ranking engine re-orders results based on what has actually worked together in past projects at your studio — surfacing furniture proven to co-exist beautifully in real interiors.
